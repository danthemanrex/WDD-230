Choose Membership

Unique to non-profits, expand your organizations reach by joining the Tri Cities Chamber to gain access to many of the valley's other top community-focused businesses.

Get Established! Bronze memberships are recommended for businesses with 1-2 employees and are intended for startups and new businesses. Bronze membership is a great stepping stone to business success. Join to begin building name recognition and rapport with other business leaders in the community.

Marketing & Networking! Silver Memberships are recommended for businesses with 2-5 employees and are for established businesses looking to grow. This level is ideal for a business wanting to gain exposure and build community awareness. Silver membership offers all of the benefits of the Bronze level, plus additional marketing tools and benefits. Silver membership includes 4 Luncheon tickets.

Business Development - Gold memberships are for established businesses working to become leaders in the Flathead Valley. Increase visibility - this level includes additional marketing opportunities. Gold membership includes 8 Luncheon tickets.
