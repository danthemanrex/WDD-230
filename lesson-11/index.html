Idaho... Where Adventures Come to Life

Let’s see Shosone Falls, Hells Canyon, Craters of the Moon Museum. Hold up! I said to my excited husband; where are they? In the middle of Montana, Wyoming, Washington and Oregon. The little “gem” state, Idaho. Of course I knew then he had gone mad. During this pandemic I wanted to travel the Oregon Trail?

He has been reading up on the state, and is all enthusiastic about it. Keeps throwing photos of ghost towns at me, mentions square dancing. I never did like square dancing, but a ghost town? I I began tto read. Silver City, Sierra Silver Mine, Gold Dredge and more. If he knew what I was reading we would be on the road in an hour.

Idaho sounds rather fascinating to me now. There are no billionaires which means they are regular folk. People from Idaho must have a hilarious sense of humor also, I do love a sense of humor.

Two rules still on their books: One, you cannot walk on the street unless you are smiling. Two, you cannot fish from a camel’s back or a giraffe’s back.

I am packed and ready in three hours. Head me toward Idaho, honey. I want to see an Appaloosa horse, mountain blue bird and cutthroat trout. Not to mention those ghost towns.

Written By Caren Krutsinger
