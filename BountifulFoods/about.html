About Us

Welcome

we're Bountiful foods, a business that loves to provide healthy food to the people of carlsbad, carlifonia and it's visitors.

Hi, we are Samuel Christensen, Bella Shmurda, and Abigail Samson, three individuals sparked with the passion for providing organic fruits, and vegetables to people since 2015, we truly believe that the availability of organic produce will bring good health to people in our community.

our mission is what drives us to do everything possible to expand the provision of organic foods to people at Carlsbad, we do that by organizing quarterly groundbreaking campaigns to elighten our people of the benefits of organic foods, we also have our web page were people can order for their fresh fruit mix and learn more about what we do.
